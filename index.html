Benefits of Regular Massage

Physical Benefits

Regular massage provides numerous physical benefits including: relaxes the body, reduces swelling, calms the nervous system, lowers blood pressure, reduces heart rate, stretches connective tissue, reduces chronic pain, improves red blood cell count, relieves tired and aching muscles, improves muscle tone, speeds recovery from energy, reduces tension headaches, increases tissue metabolism, decreases muscle deterioration, speeds recovery from illness, increases range of motion, speeds elimination of metabolic waste, slows respiration, relieves cramped muscles, increases blood and lymph circulation, stimulates release of endorphins, strengthens the immune system, improves posture, and improves skin tone.

Emotional Benefits

Massage therapy offers significant emotional benefits: reduces anxiety, relieves cramped muscles, provides a feeling of well being, enhances self-image, and nurtures and stimulates emotional growth.

Mental Benefits

Experience mental wellness through massage: reduces mental stress, promotes quality sleep, improves productivity, and induces mental relaxation.
