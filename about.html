About Healthy Touch Heals

Our Story

Welcome to Healthy Touch Heals, where healing and relaxation meet. For 30 years, we have been helping people relax and find relief from the stresses of daily life. Since 1995, we have been serving our community with dedication, expertise, and genuine care.

Our mission is simple: to provide exceptional massage therapy services that promote physical wellness, mental relaxation, and overall health. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the transformative power of therapeutic touch.

Meet Shawna Conner, LMT, BCTMB

Shawna is a Licensed Massage Therapist and Nationally Board-Certified Practitioner in Therapeutic Massage & Bodywork (BCTMB). With 30 years of experience helping people relax since 1995, Shawna brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every session.

Her approach is holistic, considering not just the physical symptoms but the overall well-being of each client. She takes the time to understand your concerns, ask insightful questions, and customize her approach to meet your unique needs. Each massage is deeply effective and restorative, tailored specifically to address your concerns.

Shawna's continuous education and training ensure that she stays current with the latest techniques and best practices in the field of massage therapy, providing you with the highest quality care.
